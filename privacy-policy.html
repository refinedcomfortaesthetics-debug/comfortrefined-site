Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us.

Last Updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Refined Comfort Aesthetics ("we," "us," or "our"), located at 1245 Gruene Rd, New Braunfels, TX 78130, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website (comfortrefined.com), book an appointment, or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information — your name, email address, and phone number when you book an appointment, fill out a contact form, or subscribe to our email list.
  • Appointment Information — service preferences and scheduling details provided when booking through our booking platform (Cal.com).
  • Email Marketing Data — your email address and communication preferences if you opt in to our Inner Circle email list through MailerLite.
  • Usage Data — anonymous information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited, time on site, and general geographic location, collected via analytics tools.
  • Communications — any messages you send us via phone, text, email, or social media.

We do not collect payment information directly. Any payments are processed securely through third-party platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Confirm and manage your appointments
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send appointment reminders and follow-up communications
  • Send you our email newsletter and exclusive offers, if you have opted in
  • Improve our website and services based on usage patterns
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Email Marketing & Opt-Out

If you subscribe to our Inner Circle email list, you consent to receiving periodic emails from Refined Comfort Aesthetics. Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You may opt out at any time at no cost. We use MailerLite to manage our email list — your data is stored and processed in accordance with MailerLite's privacy practices.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following trusted third-party services that may collect or process data on our behalf:

  • Cal.com — online appointment booking
  • MailerLite — email marketing and list management
  • Cloudflare — website security, performance, and CDN
  • Google Analytics (if enabled) — anonymous website usage statistics
  • Google Maps / Reviews — location and review links

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the data they collect. We encourage you to review their policies if you have concerns.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience and gather anonymous analytics data. Cookies set by third-party services (such as Cloudflare or analytics tools) are governed by those services' policies.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to browse our site or book appointments.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Our website is protected by Cloudflare, which provides SSL encryption and security monitoring. We limit access to your personal information to only those who need it to operate our business.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request that we not sell your information (we do not sell personal data)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

9. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as Facebook, Instagram, and Google. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
