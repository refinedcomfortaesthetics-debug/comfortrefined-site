About Courtney

Expert Care by Courtney Bush

Licensed Esthetician · Electrologist · Certified Non-Invasive Advanced Body Contouring Specialist

With over a decade of dedicated expertise in aesthetics, Courtney Bush has established herself as a leading practitioner for permanent hair removal in New Braunfels and non-invasive body sculpting for San Antonio area clients seeking lasting confidence.

After graduating from esthetics school in Dallas in 2015, Courtney added Electrology to her credentials — driven not just by professional curiosity, but by something deeply personal. Her own experience with unwanted facial hair gave her a firsthand understanding of the frustration, the temporary fixes, and the emotional weight that so many clients carry into their first appointment. That lived experience is the foundation of how she practices: with patience, empathy, and an unwavering commitment to real, lasting results.

As the founder of Refined Comfort Aesthetics, Courtney has built a boutique studio where clients are never rushed, never judged, and always heard. Whether you're managing hormonally driven hair growth, recovering confidence after a weight-loss journey, or simply ready to stop maintaining something you've always wanted gone — she creates a treatment plan tailored specifically to you.

Proudly serving New Braunfels and the Greater San Antonio area from inside Gruene Day Spa, Courtney invites you to schedule a free 30-minute consultation to begin your journey in a space designed entirely for your comfort.
